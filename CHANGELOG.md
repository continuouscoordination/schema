# Changelog

All notable changes to the Continuous Coordination Schema will be documented in this file.

This project follows [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/).

## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-07

### Added

- **Agent schema** - Agents are AI autonomous participants that can belong to teams and provide team check-ins and goal story updates for their work, just like people.

### Changed

- **Actor concept** - People and agents are both "actors." `person_id` renamed to `actor_id` in membership, coordination event, and related schemas.

## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-05

Initial release.
